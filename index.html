Tools

To make classic drinks bartenders require more than spirits mixers and the right ingredients.They need the right tools to make drinks consistent ,quicky and efficiently.

  • Bottle opener

    the most important tool for any bartender,bottle openers
    are cruicial as every beer bottle you will see will be capped.


  • Cocktail Shaker

    Ingredients are placed in the sealed stainless steel shaker, typically
    liquor, syrups, fruit juices, and ice.


  • Cocktail Strainers

    You'll need one of these to create cold ice-free drinks.
    The strainer is placed over the mouth of the shaker,with the small holes
    letting the drink pass through, leaving the ice in the shaker.


  • Jigger

    acts as a cocktail measuring cup, Jiggers are dual-sided and help
    bartenders cut down on overpouring and remain consistent .


  • Peelers

    For those who like garnishes,peelers
    are important as you can use the essential oils from orange lemon
    or limes skins for your drinks.


  • Bar Spoons

    Bar spoons gently stir the ingredients to just
    the right dilution without breaking down the ice.

Classic Cocktails

Now, what makes a classic cocktail "Classic"?

  • Old fashioned

    Old fashioned is a cocktail made by muddling sugar with bitters and adding whiskey,
    Traditionally bourbon, garnishing with an orange peel presented in a rocks glass.

    1. grab a mixing glass
    2. pour 60ml of your favourite whiskey/bourbon.
    3. pour 5ml of simple syrup or add an sugar cube inside the mixing glass
    4. add 3 dashes of angustora bitters
    5. add ice to the mixing glass, using your bar spoon start to stir the drink for about 20 seconds
    6. grab a rocks glass and add ice to it
    7. grab a strainer and place it on top of the mixing glass
    8. pour the contents to rocks glass.
    9. garnish with an orange peel.

    • Negroni

    A Negroni is an Italian cocktail, made of one part gin, one part vermouth and one
    part Campari, garnished with orange peel.

    1. grab a mixing glass
    2. pour 25ml of your favourite gin
    3. pour 25ml of Campari
    4. pour 25ml of vermouth
    5. add 3 dashes of orange bitters (optional)
    6. add ice to the mixing glass, using your bar spoon start to stir the drink for about 20 seconds
    7. grab a rocks glass and add ice to it
    8. grab a strainer and place it on top of the mixing glass
    9. pour the contents to the rocks glass
    10. garnish with a orange slice

  • cosmopolitan

    made famous from the popular show 'sex and the city' is a classic cocktail made
    with vodka,cointreau, lime juice an cranberry juice

    1. grab a cocktail shaker
    2. pour 50ml of vodka into the shaker
    3. poru 20ml of cointreau into the shaker
    4. pour 25ml lime juice into the shaker
    5. pour 50ml of cranberry juice into the shaker
    6. place the smaller on top of the bigger one and give it a hard hit to create a tight seal and start shaking for about 20 seconds
    7. grab a coupe glass
    8. place a strainer on top of the shaker and pour the contents into the coupe glass
    9. garnish with a orange peel

    Espresso Martini

    The espresso martini is a cold caffeinated alcoholic drink made with espresso,
    coffee liqueur, and vodka.

      1. grab a cocktail shaker
      2. pour 50ml of vodka into the shaker
      3. pour 25ml of coffee liqueur into the shaker
      4. pour 20ml of syimple syrup preferably vanilla syrup into the shaker
      5. pour 50ml of fresh espresso into the shaker
      6. place the smaller shaker on top of the bigger one and give it a hard hit to creat a tight seal and give it a hard shake for about 20 seconds
      7. place a strainer on top of the shaker and pour the contents into a coupe glass
      8. garnish with 3 coffee beans

      Mojito

      Mojito is a traditional Cuban punch. The cocktail often consists of five
      ingredients: white rum, sugar, lime juice, soda water, and mint

      1. grab a high ball glass
      2. place a handful of mint leaves in the glass
      3. pour 50ml of your favourite rum
      4. pour 25ml of lime juice
      5. pour 20ml of simple syrup
      6. top up the glass with crushed ice
      7. add soda water
      8. with a bar spoon mix the contents together
      9. garnish with a mint spring and a lime wheel

    1. Whiskey sour

      hiskey sour is a mixed drink containing whiskey, lemon juice, sugar,
      and optionally, a dash of egg white

      1. grab a cocktail shaker
      2. pour in 60ml of your favorite whiskey
      3. pour 25ml of lemon juice
      4. pour 15ml-20ml of simple syrup
      5. pour 26ml of egg whites or a vegan alternative 'aquafaba'
      6. without any ice in the shaker ,give it a hard shake to emulsify the egg white to create foam, this is called a dry shake.
      7. now pour ice in the shaker and shake for 20 seconds
      8. grab a rocks glass and add ice to it
      9. place a strainer on top of the shaker and pour the contents
      10. garnish with a maraschino cherry or dashes of angustora bitters

  • Margarita

    margarita is a cocktail consisting of Tequila, triple sec, and lime juice often served
    with salt on the rim of the glass.

    1. grab a cocktail shaker
    2. pour in 50ml of Tequila
    3. pour in 25ml of lime juice
    4. pour in 25ml of cointreau
    5. place ice in shaker and shake for about 20 seconds
    6. grab a coupe glass and rim the glass with salt
    7. grab a strainer and place it on top of shaker and pour contents into the coupe glass
    8. garnish with a lime wheel

  • Martini

    The martini is a cocktail made with gin and vermouth, and garnished with an olive
    or a lemon twist. Over the years, the martini has become one of the best-known
    cocktail.it is usually stirred, but also shaken if youre James Bond

    1. grab a mixing glass
    2. pour in 60ml of gin
    3. pour in 10ml of vermouth
    4. add ice to the mixing glass and stir for about 20 seconds
    5. grab a strainer and place it on top of the shaker
    6. grab a martini glass and pour the contents into it
    7. garnish with a lemon twist or olive

    What is a Mocktail?


    Mocktails are non-alcoholic mixed drinks meant to replicate the presentation and complexity of craft cocktails, just without the alcohol.To make mocktails, bartenders mix combinations of sodas, juices, herbs, and syrups to create unique flavors.They also use the same mixology methods and tools they would use with regular mixed drinks

    • Virgin Mojito

      1. grab a high ball glass
      2. place a handful of mint leaves in the glass
      3. pour 25ml of lime juice
      4. pour 20ml of simple syrup
      5. top up the glass with crushed ice
      6. add soda water
      7. with a bar spoon mix the contents together
      8. garnish with a mint spring and a lime wheel

    • Seedlip Garden Tonic

      1. grab a gin glass
      2. pour ice into the glass and add 50ml of seedlip (distilled non-alcoholic spirit)
      3. pour in 200ml of Fevertree Elderflower Tonic
      4. garnish with a lime

    • Strawberry Cream Soda

      1. grab a high ball glass
      2. pour 50ml of strawberry puree
      3. pour 25ml lemon juice
      4. pour 20ml vanilla syrup
      5. pour 75ml pineapple juice
      6. fill glass with crushed ice
      7. top up with soda water
      8. garnish with mint leaf and strawberry

    • Peach & Elderflower Iced Tea

      1. grab cocktail shakers and a highball glass
      2. pour 40ml of peach puree
      3. pour 25ml of lemon juice
      4. pour 20ml of elderflower cordial
      5. pour 100ml of tea
      6. shake for 20 seconds
      7. pour contents into glass and top up with crushed ice
      8. garnish with orange slice
