Old fashioned

Old fashioned is a cocktail made by muddling sugar with bitters and adding whiskey, Traditionally bourbon, garnishing with an orange peel presented in a rocks glass.

  1. grab a mixing glass
  2. pour 60ml of your favourite whiskey/bourbon.
  3. pour 5ml of simple syrup or add an sugar cube inside the mixing glass
  4. add 3 dashes of angustora bitters
  5. add ice to the mixing glass, using your bar spoon start to stir the drink for about 20 seconds
  6. grab a rocks glass and add ice to it
  7. grab a strainer and place it on top of the mixing glass
  8. pour the contents to rocks glass.
  9. garnish with an orange peel.
